Projects

Tao - Discord Bot

Inspired by the character Hu Tao from Genshin Impact, "Tao" is a multipurpose Discord bot that manages and moderates servers. It features dedicated music capabilities powered by Lavalink, entertainment options like a currency game, meme functionalities, and more!

Alcohol Detector Using MQ3 Sensor

Developed as a research capstone project during my senior year, this alcohol detector uses an MQ3 sensor powered by Arduino. It calculates approximate Blood Alcohol Concentration (BAC) and was awarded the best in research for its advanced PPM alcohol detection. The device was intended for use in schools to test students for alcohol consumption upon entry.
